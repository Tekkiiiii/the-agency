## Diacritics Split Strategy

### The có dấu / không dấu problem
Vietnamese users search both ways. The split is not random:
- Older users (40+): higher tendency to search không dấu on desktop
- Mobile users: predictive text usually inserts có dấu automatically
- Voice search: always có dấu (speech recognition adds diacritics)
- Zalo search: mixture (keyboard behavior varies by device)

### Recommendation
- Primary: có dấu (Google and CocCoc prefer canonical forms)
- Secondary: không dấu variant in meta description and first paragraph
- Title tags: pick one form, use consistently — never mix in same tag
- URL slugs: always không dấu (ASCII), properly transliterated (thành công → thanh-cong, never thnh-cng)

## Regional Keyword Splitting

Northern vs Southern vocabulary differences affect SEO. Key pairs:
- bắp (S) / ngô (N) — corn
- heo (S) / lợn (N) — pork
- ba (S) / bố (N) — father
- mền (S) / chăn (N) — blanket
- thơm (S) / dứa (N) — pineapple
- bịch (S) / túi nilông (N) — plastic bag

National campaigns: include both forms as keyword variants.
Regional targeting: use dominant regional term.

## CocCoc Optimization

- CocCoc holds ~30% of Vietnam browser market share
- Uses its own crawl index — does not mirror Google rankings
- CocCoc-specific signals: Vietnamese content volume, .vn TLD, Vietnamese user engagement
- CocCoc Shopping tab: product title conventions differ from Google Shopping
- Less emphasis on backlink authority, more on content freshness and Vietnamese-language relevance
- Recommendation: optimize for Google first, verify CocCoc separately

## Conversion Copywriting Patterns

### Action verb frontloading by intent
- Informational: "Cách [X]", "Hướng dẫn [X]", "Tại sao [X]"
- Commercial: "Mua [X] ở đâu", "Giá [X] bao nhiêu", "[X] tốt nhất 2026"
- Transactional: "Đặt [X]", "Mua [X]", "Tải [X]"
- Navigational: "[Tên thương hiệu] + [sản phẩm]"

## Social Proof Anchors in Vietnamese

Vietnamese audiences trust peer validation more than expert ratings for B2C:
- Number anchors: "X người đã dùng" > "Đánh giá 4.8 sao"
- Community: "Hội chị em bỉm sữa đang dùng" > "Top 1 thị trường"
- Local authority: VnExpress/Tuổi Trẻ feature > international awards
- Shopee/Tiki star ratings outperform testimonial text

## Long-form vs Short-form by Platform

| Platform | Optimal length | Format | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Google Blog | 1,200–2,500 words | H2/H3, FAQ at end | Organic traffic |
| CocCoc | 800–1,500 words | Higher keyword density | VN traffic |
| Facebook | 300–800 words | Story narrative, no headers | Engagement |
| TikTok caption | 80–120 chars | Hook only | Watch time |
| Email newsletter | 200–400 words | 1-idea emails | Click-through |
| Zalo broadcast | 100–200 chars | Direct, no narrative | Action |

## Vietnamese Blog/Article Structure

Aligned with deductive preference:
1. H1: Keyword-led, addresses user's question directly
2. Introduction (100–150 words): state answer immediately, then contextualize
3. Table of contents (for >1,000 words — Vietnamese readers expect it)
4. H2 sections: each addresses one subtopic
5. FAQ section: 4–6 questions matching common voice search queries
6. Conclusion with CTA
7. Related posts / See also

### Meta Description Conventions
- Vietnamese: 120–140 characters (shorter than English due to character width)
- Include primary keyword in có dấu form
- End with soft CTA verb
