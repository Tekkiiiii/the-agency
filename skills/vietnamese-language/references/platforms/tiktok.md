## Challenge Vocabulary

- Standard framing: "#[TênChallenge]Challenge" — verb-based names outperform noun-based
- Participation CTA: "Mọi người thử challenge này và tag mình xem nha!"
- Response video: "Mình cũng thử challenge [tên] xem thế nào"
- Duet invitation: "Ai muốn duet với mình thì comment nha"
- Branded challenge naming: keep brand name optional — generic-sounding names get wider participation

## Duet and Stitch Language

- Position framing: "Ý kiến của mình là..." (My take is...)
- Culturally safe disagreement: "Mình nghĩ hơi khác một chút..." — never confrontational
- Agreement amplification: "Thật ra bạn ấy đúng vì..." (Actually they're right because...)
- Stitch context: "Mình stitch video này vì..." (I'm stitching this because...)
- Reaction register: match or slightly exceed the energy of the original video

## FYP Optimization Hooks

- First sentence must contain a question or bold claim to trigger VN algorithmic distribution
- First-3-second structures proven in VN: "Bạn có biết là..." (Did you know...), "Đừng bao giờ..." (Never...), "Tôi đã mất [X] để..." (I lost [X] to...)
- Comment-bait for saves: "Lưu lại vì kiểu gì cũng cần" (Save this — you'll need it)
- Save-trigger: instructional content with "Bước 1... Bước 2..." signals save-worthy
- Retention hook: mid-video "Đợi đến cuối nhé" (Wait until the end) keeps watch time up

## Trending Audio Integration

- Lip-sync: overlay text with key message, not full transcription
- Sound-reaction framing: "Khi nghe bài này mình nghĩ đến..." (When I hear this I think of...)
- Credit conventions: tag original audio creator in caption — VN community values attribution
- Vpop and K-pop remixes outperform US audio for VN audience reach
- Original audio with voiceover: strongest for educational/authority content

## Creator-Brand Collab Language

- Disclosure: "#quangcao" or "#hoptac" in caption (not English "#ad")
- Integration: "Mình hợp tác với [brand]" — transparent, peer voice
- Product demonstration: show usage first, brand mention after value delivery
- Authenticity signal: "Mình dùng thật rồi mới giới thiệu" (I actually used it before recommending)
- Over-produced KOC content destroys authenticity — keep it natural
