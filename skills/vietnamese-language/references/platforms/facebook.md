## Group Dynamics

- Comment culture in buy/sell groups: seller register uses "Inbox mình nha anh/chị" (not "DM me"), buyer confirms with "Chốt" or "Lấy"
- Seeding language: personal narrative voice wins in groups — "Mình vừa dùng thử và thấy..." (I just tried and found...)
- Trust signals specific to VN groups: offering Zalo number for direct contact, video proof of product, showing "đã bán X đơn" (sold X orders), live unboxing
- Moderation-aware language: use "hỗ trợ" not "chữa" in health/beauty groups to avoid keyword flags
- Group admin relationship matters: getting pinned by admin = massive credibility boost

## Livestream Seller Language

- Opening hooks: "Chào cả nhà! Hôm nay mình có deal cực nóng cho anh chị nè!"
- The "mời anh chị" cadence: repeat invitations to comment/order throughout stream — "Ai lấy comment số 1 nha!"
- Flash sale countdown: "Còn đúng 5 phần thôi, ai nhanh thì chốt trước nha!"
- Gift incentive: "Share livestream thì được tặng thêm [quà] luôn nè"
- Off-air follow-up: always redirect to Zalo for closing — "Inbox mình để nhận thông tin chi tiết nha"
- Price reveal technique: build value first, delay price to end of segment — "Giá chị em đoán bao nhiêu?"

## Age-Segment Register Differences

- **18–25**: Peer register (bạn/mình), English loanwords fine, meme references expected, emoji-heavy, informal grammar acceptable
- **26–35**: Bạn/mình register, more formal CTAs, trust signals matter (reviews, certificates), mixed Vietnamese-English OK
- **36–50**: Formal register even on Facebook, avoid slang entirely, family/health hooks land strongest, full sentences expected
- **50+**: Full formal (anh/chị/quý vị), no loanwords, national pride and community references, no emoji, respectful tone mandatory

## Marketplace Language

- Product listing: price at top, condition language ("mới 100%", "like new 99%", "đã sử dụng")
- Negotiation: "Giá fix hay còn thương lượng ạ?" (Fixed or negotiable?)
- C2C trust: "Ship COD, kiểm hàng trước khi thanh toán" (COD, inspect before paying)
- Location convention: always district + city ("Quận 7, TP.HCM")
- Urgency: "Cần bán gấp" (Need to sell urgently) signals real seller, not brand

## Comment-as-Content Strategy

- Detailed replies boost reach more than the original caption
- Reply register: match the commenter's register (if they use "chị" → reply with "chị")
- Pin informational comment with product details to avoid link-in-post reach suppression
- Encourage threads: "Comment số 1 để nhận [offer]" — drives engagement metrics
- Respond to negative comments with empathy first: "Cảm ơn anh/chị đã phản hồi, [tên] xin ghi nhận ạ"
