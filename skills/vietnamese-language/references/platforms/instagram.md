## Aesthetic Caption Conventions

- Caption-as-poem: short lines, emotional rhythm — "Sáng nay. Cà phê. Và mình." (lifestyle/travel)
- Caption-as-story: narrative arc with personal insight (education/personal brand)
- Punctuation rhythm: ellipsis (...) used for pacing, not trailing off
- Hashtag placement: VN convention is first comment (not end of caption) — keeps caption clean

## Carousel Education Format

- Slide 1: hook as question or bold claim ("3 sai lầm khi...")
- Slide structure: numbered with Vietnamese headers ("Bước 1:", "Sai lầm #2:")
- Final slide: call-to-save "Lưu lại để dùng khi cần" + follow CTA
- Text: short phrases designed for swipe reading, not paragraphs

## Story Interaction Language

- Poll framing: culturally resonant binary choices ("Cà phê sáng hay trà chiều?")
- Q&A sticker: "Hỏi mình bất cứ điều gì — mình trả lời thật lòng"
- Quiz: trivia about Vietnamese culture or niche expertise performs best
- Countdown sticker: "Còn X ngày nữa" for launches/events

## Collab Post Language

- Introducing: "Cùng với @[creator] chia sẻ [topic]"
- Register: match both creators' tone — hybrid voice must feel natural
- Cross-promotion: tag in caption + Story mention with personal recommendation

## Reels vs Feed Strategy

- Reels: entertainment-first, trend-driven, wider discovery reach
- Feed/Carousel: education-first, save-driven, stronger with existing followers
- VN IG audience skews younger and more aesthetic-conscious than Facebook
