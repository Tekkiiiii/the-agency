# Threads VN — Cultural Behavior Patterns

> Slow-growing platform in Vietnam. Primary users: Gen Z creators, Instagram-adjacent audience.

## Creator-first Register

- More conversational and experimental than Instagram VN
- Tone: casual, opinion-forward — "Mình đang suy nghĩ về..." (I'm thinking about...)
- Less polished than IG captions — authenticity over aesthetics

## Conversation Patterns

- Reply invitation: "Mọi người nghĩ sao?" (What does everyone think?)
- Multi-post threading: numbered thoughts (1/, 2/, 3/) for longer ideas
- Creator-to-creator engagement is the primary growth mechanism
- Repost with comment: add personal take when sharing another creator's thread
