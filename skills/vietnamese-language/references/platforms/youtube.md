# YouTube VN — Cultural Behavior Patterns

> Supplements content-creator/references/platforms.md (format specs).

## Title Optimization

- Front-load keywords: Vietnamese word order differs from Google search — test both orders
- Emotional brackets: [SỰ THẬT], [CHIA SẺ], [CẢNH BÁO] — effective sparingly, overuse = spam
- Click-through phrases in VN: "mà ít ai biết", "bạn cần biết", "sự thật về"
- Number-anchored: "Top 5...", "3 cách..." — numbered titles outperform
- Avoid clickbait that doesn't deliver — VN audience aggressively dislikes broken promises

## Description Conventions

- First 2 lines visible without expanding — put key info there
- Timestamp labeling: "00:00 Giới thiệu / 02:30 Bước 1: [topic] / ..." in Vietnamese
- Links section: "🔗 Liên kết hữu ích:" header, then labeled links
- Hashtags: 3-5 at end of description (not in title)

## Comment Engagement Patterns

- Creator response: warm, personal, match commenter's tone ("Cảm ơn bạn nha!")
- Pin informational comment: "📌 Thông tin bổ sung: ..."
- Community posts: casual, conversational, poll-driven
- Membership language: "Gia đình [Channel Name]" — collectivist framing

## Thumbnail Text Conventions

- Vietnamese text overlay: max 4-6 words, bold, high contrast
- Emotional face + text works across demographics
- No full sentences — thumbnail text is a headline

## Shorts vs Long-form

- Shorts: discovery tool, TikTok-style hooks, drives subscribers
- Long-form (10-20 min): education, reviews, vlogs — VN audience has high watch time for quality
- Mixed strategy: Shorts as trailer for long-form works well in VN
