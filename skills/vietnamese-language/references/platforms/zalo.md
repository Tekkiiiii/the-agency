# Zalo OA — Cultural Behavior Patterns

> For Zalo broadcast STRATEGY and KPIs → marketing/14-email-marketing/.
> This file covers language conventions and register norms.

## Broadcast Copy

- ZNS messages: 100–200 characters max body before link
- Greeting: "Chào [Anh/Chị] [tên]!" — personalization is standard, not optional
- Personalization tokens: tên (name), ngày sinh (birthday), số đơn hàng (order number)
- One action per broadcast: never ask for two things in one message
- Read time is immediate (mobile push): write for instant comprehension, no narrative buildup
- Frequency: more than 3 per week = high unfollow risk

## Mini App Flow Language

- Navigation: "Bấm vào [nút] để [hành động]" (Tap [button] to [action])
- Error state: "Đã có lỗi xảy ra. Vui lòng thử lại sau ạ."
- Success: "Thành công! [Chi tiết hành động]."
- Loading: "Đang xử lý, vui lòng đợi..."

## Customer Service Register

- Opening: "Chào [Anh/Chị], [Tên shop] xin lắng nghe ạ."
- Always end with "ạ" — marks respectful register in CS
- Self-reference: brand name, not "tôi" or "mình"
- Customer: "Anh/Chị" — NEVER "bạn" (signals disrespect in CS)
- Late response (>2 hours): "Xin lỗi [Anh/Chị] vì phản hồi chậm. [Tên] xin hỗ trợ ạ."
- Resolution: "Đã xử lý xong cho [Anh/Chị] rồi ạ. Còn gì [Tên] hỗ trợ thêm không ạ?"
- Escalation: "Vấn đề này [Tên] sẽ chuyển lên bộ phận [X]. [Anh/Chị] vui lòng đợi trong [thời gian] ạ."

## Boss/Parent Communication Norms

- Zalo has different register than Messenger — used for boss→employee and parent→child
- Family groups: formal-polite even among siblings when parents present
- Professional-personal boundary: contacts see real name and phone — content appropriate for mixed audiences
- Work groups: always formal, titles used, no stickers/emoji in serious contexts

## Zalo OA vs Zalo Personal

- OA: brand voice, structured messages, broadcast capability, customer service
- Personal: relationship-based selling, more casual, "chị ơi" register acceptable
- Strategy: OA for acquisition/retention, personal for VIP customer nurture
