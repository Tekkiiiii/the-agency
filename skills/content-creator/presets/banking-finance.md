# Preset — Banking & Finance

Loaded only when `preset=banking-finance`. Layers finance-specific angles, hooks, trust accelerators, pricing patterns, and **strict** compliance reminders on top of the framework.

**Compliance is the boss in this preset.** Every draft must surface a "legal review required before publishing" flag.

---

## Niche Coverage

- **Retail banking** — checking, savings, debit, ATM/branch, mobile banking
- **Neobanks / digital banks** — fully digital onboarding, often with creator-style marketing
- **Lending** — personal loans, auto, mortgage, BNPL, business lending
- **Credit cards** — rewards, cashback, travel, business
- **Insurance** — life, health, auto, home, travel, pet, disability
- **Wealth management & investing** — robo-advisors, brokerages, retirement, ETFs/mutual funds
- **Payments / fintech infra** — POS, payment rails, embedded finance, payroll
- **SME finance** — business banking, invoicing, expense, working capital

---

## Audience Archetypes

- **First-time saver** — young, low confidence, learning the basics. Wants reassurance and simple language.
- **Debt-burdened** — anxious, often hiding the problem. Wants empathy + concrete steps.
- **Wealth-builder** — 30s–50s, accumulating, comparison-shopping. Values transparency, ROI math, and tax efficiency.
- **Retiree / pre-retiree** — risk-averse, capital preservation, fiduciary trust. Skeptical of hype.
- **SME owner / founder** — time-poor, mistrusts banks. Wants product that actually works for cash flow reality.
- **Mass-affluent** — $100K–$1M investable, wants white-glove without private-bank price.
- **Gen-Z digital-native** — comfortable with neobanks, learns from finfluencers, low patience for forms.

Match angle to anxiety level. Finance copy lives or dies on perceived trustworthiness.

## Finance-Specific Hooks

- "If you're 30 and have $X saved, here's the catch-up math."
- "The fee that's quietly costing you $Y/year."
- "Three reasons your savings rate isn't actually beating inflation."
- "What your bank doesn't print on the statement."
- "I audited 47 [household type] budgets. Same five mistakes every time."
- "Why your mortgage isn't the problem. It's [thing]."
- "The retirement number nobody tells you about."
- "If your APR is above [number], stop reading and call them."
- "How [country]'s tax code rewards people who [behavior]."
- "Stop putting [size] amounts in [account type]. Here's where they should go."

**Avoid hooks built around guaranteed return or "secret" — these read as scam.**

## Trust Accelerators

Required, not optional:

- **Regulator licensing** — name the regulator and license number where allowed.
  - US: state DFS, OCC, FDIC, NCUA, FINRA, SEC
  - UK: FCA, PRA
  - EU: ECB, BaFin (DE), AMF (FR), CySEC (CY)
  - Singapore: MAS
  - Vietnam: SBV (Ngân Hàng Nhà Nước Việt Nam)
  - HK: HKMA, SFC
  - Japan: JFSA
- **Insurance / protection statements** — FDIC ($250K), SIPC, FSCS, CDIC, PIDM. Be precise about coverage scope.
- **Audit / rating agency mentions** — Moody's, S&P, Fitch (institutional); Big Four auditor (corporate).
- **Security certifications** — SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, PCI DSS Level 1.
- **Executive bios with credentials** — CFA, CPA, CAIA, MBA from named institution.
- **Transparent fee tables** — published, accessible, honest. "Fee schedule" that requires login = trust killer.
- **Years in operation / customer count** — concrete, verifiable.
- **Press / awards** — only tier-1 (FT, WSJ, Bloomberg, The Banker, Forbes Best Banks).

Avoid: vague "industry-leading," anonymous testimonials, photos of stock-image executives.

## Pricing Patterns

- **APR / APY transparency** — must be exact and current. Round-up or down is fraud.
- **Fee comparison anchors** — "$0 monthly fee" anchored against industry $12 average.
- **No-fee bundles** — "Premium tier waives all transfer fees" — only if it actually does.
- **Reward-rate stacking** — "Earn X% cashback on Y, Z% on W, base 1% on everything else."
- **Intro-rate vs ongoing-rate honesty** — show both: "0% APR for 15 months, then 19.99%–28.99% APR (variable)."
- **Tiered savings APY** — show full ladder.
- **No charm pricing on premium** — "$2,000/yr" beats "$1,999/yr" for wealth management.
- **Clear effective date** — rates change; surface "rates as of [date]" in copy.

- **Trusted Advisor** — primary; works for nearly all finance content
- **Confident Expert** — for thought leadership, investing newsletters, B2B

## Compliance Reminders — STRICT

**Every draft must include the legal-review flag at the bottom.** Finance content carries personal liability for the content owner in many jurisdictions.

**Universal rules**
- Never use "guaranteed" returns, "risk-free" outside actual FDIC/SIPC/FSCS scope, "no risk," "can't lose."
- APR/APY must be exact, current, and dated.
- All promotions need disclosures of terms, fees, eligibility.
- "Advice" wording can trigger fiduciary obligations — use "information," "education," "guidance from your advisor."
- Never imply outcomes ("you'll save $X") without "may," "could," "depending on."
- Never compare to competitors by name without legal review.
- Past performance disclosure required when discussing investment returns.
- Influencer/finfluencer endorsements need disclosures (FTC #ad in US, FCA/PRA in UK).

**United States**
- **Regulation DD** (Truth in Savings) — APY disclosures
- **Regulation Z** (Truth in Lending) — APR + total cost of credit
- **TILA-RESPA / TRID** — mortgage marketing
- **FINRA Rule 2210** — broker-dealer communications
- **SEC Marketing Rule** — investment adviser communications
- **FTC Endorsement Guides** — influencers + customer testimonials
- **State insurance commissioners** — insurance ad approval, state-by-state

**EU**
- **MiFID II** — investment marketing communications
- **PRIIPs** — KIDs for packaged investments
- **EU Consumer Credit Directive** — APR + standardized info
- **GDPR** — customer-data references in marketing

**United Kingdom**
- **FCA Handbook (CONC, COBS, BCOBS)** — communications must be "clear, fair, not misleading"
- **Financial promotion regime** — strict rules on who can promote what

**Vietnam**
- **SBV (Ngân Hàng Nhà Nước)** — banking products
- **Bộ Tài Chính** — insurance and securities
- **Avoid**: "lãi suất cao nhất thị trường" (without proof), "đảm bảo lợi nhuận," "không rủi ro"
- Required: rate effective date, T&C link, regulator info

**Singapore / HK / SEA / Other markets** — defer to local regulator requirements; never publish without local legal sign-off.

**Always add to the draft (verbatim)**:

> ⚠️ **Legal review required before publishing.** This draft contains regulated financial content. Verify rate accuracy, disclosure completeness, and jurisdiction-specific compliance with your legal/compliance team. Do not publish without sign-off.
